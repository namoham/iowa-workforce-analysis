# Iowa Industry Wage & Employment Analysis

**Tools:** SQL (SQLite), Power BI  
**Data Source:** Iowa QCEW — data.gov (Quarterly Census of Employment and Wages)  
**Date:** June 2026

## Project Overview
End-to-end analysis of Iowa's industry-level employment and wage trends (2022-2024), 
identifying high-growth sectors, wage premiums, and economic structure.

## Dashboard Pages
- **Industry Overview:** Top-paying industries, wage vs employment positioning
- **Growth Analysis:** Fastest-growing industries by employment and wages
- **Economic Structure:** Employment and wage share by sector

## Key Findings
- **Securities & Financial Services** lead wages at $2,629/week
- **Tech sectors** (Computer Systems, Computing Infrastructure) show strong wages ($2,320-$2,304/week)
- **Educational Services** recovered strongly post-pandemic (+43% employment)
- **Energy sectors** (Petroleum, Pipeline) saw strongest wage growth (+25-31%)
- **Manufacturing** captures 8.2% of wages despite 6.4% employment — high-value sector

## SQL Skills Demonstrated
- Complex CTEs and window functions (LAG, OVER)
- Aggregation and grouping
- Percentage calculations and variance analysis
- Data quality filtering (sanity checks for anomalies)
